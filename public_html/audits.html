Energy Audits Need an Update

Owning and maintaining a home requires a ton of work, and when it comes to our energy bills, a ton of money too. There are tons of options out there to help you lower your energy bill but its hard to know what is worth the investment. That's where we come in. Energy audits help you determine how best to invest your money into your home, but they usually require someone coming to your home and spending hours taking measurements and working with you. The IBN energy audit process uses innovation, user input, and automation to save you both time and money. Let's bring energy audits in to the 21st century.
