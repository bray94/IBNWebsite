We believe cheap energy should be for everyone.

Energy isn't just a concern for a small group of people, it's the biggest issue for everyone. Cheap, abundant energy is at the root of most of the problems we face today. With limitless, free energy we could grow a surplus of food or build houses for everyone in the world. We could revolutionize every industry, but these things require an immense amount of energy. We are making it our personal mission to expand the renewable energy market to as many people as we can. No one should be denied the opporunity to go green. This isn't an easy problem to solve and one established companies have grappled with for years, but we have some incredibly bright and passionate minds working on it.
