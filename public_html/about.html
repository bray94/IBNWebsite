Meet the people changing the solar market.
We believe cheap energy should be for everyone.

Energy isn't just a concern for a small group of people, it's the biggest issue for everyone. Cheap, abundant energy is at the root of most of the problems we face today. With limitless, free energy we could grow a surplus of food or build houses for everyone in the world. We could revolutionize every industry, but these things require an immense amount of energy. We are making it our personal mission to expand the renewable energy market to as many people as we can. No one should be denied the opportunity to go green. This isn't an easy problem to solve and one established companies have grappled with for years, but we have some incredibly bright and passionate minds working on it.

The Team

We have a talented group of people tackling this problem. We all stem from the University of Illinois in Urbana-Champaign with a wide variety of technical backgrounds. We have expertise in electrical engineering, physics, materials, chemical engineering, computer science, brodcasting, marketing, and more just between the four of us. We are passionate about this problem. We all come from communities who want solar, but can't necessarily afford it. We vow to push clean energy everywhere we can.
