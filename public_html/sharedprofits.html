Let's Share The Profits

Joining our Shared Profits Program™ allows you to get paid for going solar. How? By joining the shared profits program you can take advantage of tax incentives, energy refund checks, and other financial benefits. Simply give us your consent to allow IBN to use your roof and/or backyard to collect solar power. That's it. All of this with no cost or risk to you. While we are working to make this project a reality, we ask you take our 2-minute survey below to help us prove that there is a need for this program.
