Let's Share The Profits

Joining our Shared Profits Program™ allows you to get paid for going solar. How? Just let IBN use part of your roof and/or backyard space to collect solar power and we will share part of the revenue with you. This includes the tax incentives and energy credits usually withheld from the consumer. That's it. All of this with no cost or risk to you. While we are working to make this project a reality, we ask you to take our 2-minute survey below to help us prove that there is a need for this program.

What makes the Shared Profits Program™ different from other leasing programs?

Purchasing solar panels outright can be very expensive, and leasing them from a company can cause more of a headache than it's worth. We wanted to find somewhere in between these two solutions that just worked simpler. We wanted something that would help anyone go green while putting a little extra cash in their pocket. We accomplished this by creating the Shared Profits Program.

This program requires only one thing from you, your permission to let us use your solar resource. No money down. No changing energy companies. No Hassle. We sell this energy back to the utility company for a profit. In exchange for you letting us use your space, we cut you a percentage of the revenue from the installed panel. It's that simple. Below are a few of the notable comparisons with traditional lease programs.
