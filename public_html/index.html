What if you could get paid for reducing your carbon footprint?

Joining our shared profits program allows you to get paid for going solar. How? By joining the shared profits program you can take advantage of tax incentives, energy refund checks, consumer convenience, and no risk. You allow us to make solar available to you, and in return you make a little extra cash. All of this with no upfront cost to you.

Interest Survey

The Shared Profits Program in a Nutshell:

  • We pay for the solar panel and installation, so there is no out-of-pocket cost for you to become a member.
  • Members are guaranteed a yearly Energy Refund Check up to 50% of the solar net profit (~$300-$800).
  • Energy Refund Check yearly amount is dependent on cost of electricity, size of installation, and geographic location.
  • Solar panel buy-out option for members of over 5 consecutive years, which allows homeowners to get 100% of the solar net profit minus a small maintenance fee.
  • Members get a cash signing bonus for enrollment in the program.
  • Members receive reduced prices, up to 50% off, for home energy audits and other IBN sponsored products/services.
  • Emergency Solar energy will be available during times of power outage.
  • Does not affect relationship with utility company.

Requirements for Shared Profits Program:

  • Must be an homeowner
  • Must have roof or yard space available for solar panel
  • Sign 6-year contract for membership
  • No credit scrore required

By indicating that you're interested in the Shared Profits Program on the survey to the right you are NOT required to enroll in the program.
